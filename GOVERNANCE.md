# AI-card Governance 

## Guiding principles:

Community-driven, neutral, transparent
Decisions based on consensus and technical merit

## Roles
### Contributors
Anyone who makes a contribution.
Rights: propose changes, open issues, submit PRs.

## Committers
Contributors with a history of quality work.
Responsibilities: review/merge PRs, maintain code.
Added by nomination + lazy consensus of existing Committers.
Decision Making
Slow consensus (default): silence = agreement after 7 days. 
Two Maintainer Approvals (LGTM x2) required for merges
Vote (fallback): majority for normal decisions, 2/3 for major changes.

## RFC Process
Changes require an RFC in a `/design` or `/docs/proposals` directory.
Open public discussion
Must receive LGTM from two Maintainers
Major RFCs (such as breaking changes) require a simple majority Maintainer vote

## Licensing
Apache 2.0
DCO instead of a CLA 

## Governance Changes
Require a public proposal + 2/3 Committer approval.
